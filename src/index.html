Introduction to the idea.

Real-time visit tracking.

Coin earnings for each action (QR scan, quiz, etc.).

Personalized rewards via AI.

Smart loyalty program.

Visit a specific enclosure.

Participate in a mini-game or quiz.

Share a photo on social media.

Participate in an activity or feed the animals.
